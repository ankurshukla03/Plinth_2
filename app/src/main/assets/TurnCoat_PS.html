Format
  1. It is an individual event.
  2. The speakers will have 3 minutes as speaking time.
  3. The switch over between the two parts must be smooth and fluid.
  4. The speaker can choose either English or Hindi as a medium.
  5. The motions will be closed and not open to any interpretation.
  6. All the motions will be given on the spot. Preparation time of 15 minutes will be given.
