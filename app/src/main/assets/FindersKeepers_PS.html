Evaluation Criteria

The Team who successfully solves the final clue will be the winner. Only the first three teams would qualify for the prizes.

Format
  1. Three people have to form a team.
  2. All the teams will be given a puzzle to solve.
  3. Teams who solve the puzzle will be given the first clue.
  4. One clue leads to another, and the final clue will be again a puzzle.
