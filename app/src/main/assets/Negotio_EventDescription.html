Round 1 :

In this Round teams will have to submit a written document describing the problem being solved and a brief summary of the idea . The teams will be judged by the panel of mentors and the shortlisted teams will then be eligible for the 2nd round.

Last day to apply is 5th Jan 2015

Round 2 :

Among'st all the startups entries received top 25 startups would be asked to present a Business model canvas about the idea within 7 days. During this period the top 25 startups will get extensive online mentoring support from elite panel of mentors of IvyCamp & Inspidate so as to furnish and polish their idea to create the perfect canvas.

Round 3:

A total of 10 entries would be shortlisted and these winners from the second round will be invited to LNMIIT campus for the final pitching round in front of investors , accelerators, incubators and idea backers.
Final round will comprise of presentation and Q-n-A with judges and representatives from funding agencies and incubator organizations.
Top two teams will be selected as winners and runners-up respectively from this round and will be provided with cash prize and many other kind of incentives.
