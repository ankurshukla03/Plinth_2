General Rules
  1. Participants are required to register and submit their questionnaire/ business model online.
  2. A team can have one or more members with a maximum of 4 members in a team. 'Startup Name' cannot be changed over the course of the competition. It should be used for any future correspondence or queries.
  3. A team can submit more than one business model/idea but in doing so, it must submit separately for each submission. The same email-id and/or startup name cannot be used for doing so.
  4. Every contestant must agree with the rules of the contest and must accept the declaration of confidentiality during submission in order to participate.
  5. Team who fail to comply to the deadline of submission of B-plan or further details beforehand would be disqualified.
  6. No startup older than 2-years would be allowed to participate.
